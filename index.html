About Vision Lab

Vision Lab focuses on fundamental and applied research in computer vision, machine learning, image processing, and signal processing. Our work covers low-shot learning, self-supervised representation, object detection, multi-camera surveillance, 3D reconstruction, and more.

We collaborate with industry and academia to bring cutting-edge algorithms into real-world applications such as robotics, intelligent surveillance, medical imaging, and autonomous systems.
